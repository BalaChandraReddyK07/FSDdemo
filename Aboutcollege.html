About SWETHA Institute of Technology & Science

 

Vision
Our vision is 'To be the best' and to 'give the best'. We have ensured that the students entering our portals evolve as the best professionals of this country. At Swetha Engineering College, you will find the pursuit of perfection alive, both in spirit and deed. Our commitment to bring affordable high quality education has set us on a glorious march towards perfection and beyond. .


Mission
The 21st century demands a knowledge-based society. As knowledge is power, it helps us to empower ourselves to succeed in the art of leading a quality life. The Institution is committed to offer modern, wholesome and quality professional-oriented education in Engineering Technology. The mission, apart from quality education, is to produce new generation engineers who are resourceful and responsible, while being socially conscious and competent enough to excel in today's demanding technological world. .
